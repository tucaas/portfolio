The Client

Iris Palo is a tattoo artist and illustrator based in Florianópolis, Brazil. Her work focuses on custom-made tattoos, developed individually for each client, as well as fine-line illustrations and personal art pieces.

She needed a visual identity and website that could better communicate her artistic style, showcase her tattoo work, and differentiate her brand from other studios in the region.

Another important goal was to clearly present her tattoo process and customer service approach, helping potential clients feel confident before getting in touch.

Target Audience

The project was designed primarily for adults between 18 and 35 years old, without restrictions related to gender.

The audience includes both first-time tattoo clients and people looking for more artistic, personalized tattoo experiences.

Visual Style

The visual direction follows a maximalist and art nouveau–inspired aesthetic, combining ornamental shapes, organic curves, and detailed decorative elements.

The goal was to create a striking and recognizable identity that reflects Iris’s artistic references while standing out from more minimal or generic tattoo studio branding.

Branding

The branding process began with the creation of the logo, combining an icon and a wordmark.

The typography was custom-designed, featuring strong curves and decorative details inspired by art nouveau references and organic floral forms.

Special attention was given to letter spacing and balance to ensure readability while maintaining a distinctive and artistic appearance.

Different logo variations were created to ensure flexibility across multiple contexts, including social media, banners, profile pictures, and headers.

These variations allow the brand to remain consistent while adapting to different formats and sizes.

Design System

A design system was developed to ensure visual consistency across all brand touchpoints.

The color palette is based on neutral tones combined with gold accents, reinforcing an elegant and artistic atmosphere inspired by classic tattoo aesthetics.

Custom icons and decorative patterns were created using elements derived from the logo, allowing the identity to extend naturally throughout the interface and printed materials.

Typography choices were carefully selected to balance expressive titles with highly readable body text.

Web Design

The website design process started with a simple one-page layout intended to present Iris’s work and explain her tattoo process.

As the project evolved, the structure expanded to include more content, allowing visitors to explore artwork, learn about the artist, and understand how to book a session.

The final layout emphasizes strong visual storytelling, using large imagery, decorative elements, and clear navigation to guide users through the page.

Interactions and content placement were designed to reinforce the brand identity while keeping the experience intuitive and accessible.
